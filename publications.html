Information Sciences, 2022

Differentially Private Synthetic Medical Data Generation using Convolutional GANs

Amirsina Torfi, Edward A. Fox, Chandan K. Reddy

Proposes a framework for generating synthetic medical data with formal differential privacy guarantees using convolutional GANs. The approach enables sharing of realistic medical datasets while protecting patient privacy, addressing a critical bottleneck in healthcare AI research.

arXiv, 2020

Natural Language Processing Advancements By Deep Learning: A Survey

Amirsina Torfi, Rouzbeh A. Shirvani, Yaser Keneshloo, Nader Tavaf, Edward A. Fox

A comprehensive survey covering the evolution of deep learning techniques in NLP, from word embeddings and recurrent architectures to transformer-based models. Reviews key advances in machine translation, text classification, question answering, and language generation.

380+ citations
IEEE Access, 2017

3D Convolutional Neural Networks for Audio-Visual Recognition

Amirsina Torfi, Seyed Mehdi Iranmanesh, Nasser M. Nasrabadi, Jeremy Dawson

Introduces a 3D CNN architecture for joint audio-visual recognition, leveraging spatiotemporal features from synchronized audio and video streams. Demonstrates effectiveness on speaker verification and lip-reading tasks with end-to-end training.
